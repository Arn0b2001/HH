Verifying Your Property

Guests shouldn't have to worry that a property is fake or isn't where it says it is. That's why we've built a new system to verify listings on HomeScape. To verify a property, hosts will need to demonstrate that the property is a real home or hotel, at an accurate location, and that they have access to that location. Now, hosts can easily do this verification by providing documentation, photos and videos.

Why we verify properties

Verifying properties helps us to scan for fraudulent behavior and makes it easier for guests to book with confidence and locate their listing upon arrival.
